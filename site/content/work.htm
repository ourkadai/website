Database Migration Flutter Mobile

EZee Infotech

Ezee Infotech is the custodian of the critical business data of privately-owned transport and logistics service providers in the South India ably steered by Jawahar Ramasamy. He was facing a unique challenge in managing the ever-growing size of the database which is swelling in size and becoming a zombie. It is impossible for him to have any downtime of the database instances due to the mission critical nature of the business data being handled.
OurKadai addressed the database issues by seamlessly migrating it to a partitioned database with zero downtime impact to the business putting Zero Downtime Indexing (ZDI) in practice which we presented as a concept in a technical conference where we met Jawahar.
Customer satisfaction is measured by the recurring business. We are proud to be on our client’s top-of-the-mind recall list whenever they face challenges. Latest challenge we are working along with Ezee Infotech is for creating Flutter based mobile applications.
Such mutual trusts that are garnered in our engagements make the technical partnership everlasting and endearing.
