Database Migration Flutter Mobile

EZee Infotech

Ezee Infotech is the custodian of the critical business data of privately-owned transport and logistics service providers in the South India ably steered by Jawahar Ramasamy. He was facing a unique challenge in managing the ever-growing size of the database which is swelling in size and becoming a zombie. It is impossible for him to have any downtime of the database instances due to the mission critical nature of the business data being handled.
OurKadai addressed the database issues by seamlessly migrating it to a partitioned database with zero downtime impact to the business putting Zero Downtime Indexing (ZDI) in practice which we presented as a concept in a technical conference where we met Jawahar.
Customer satisfaction is measured by the recurring business. We are proud to be on our client’s top-of-the-mind recall list whenever they face challenges. Latest challenge we are working along with Ezee Infotech is for creating Flutter based mobile applications.
Such mutual trusts that are garnered in our engagements make the technical partnership everlasting and endearing.

Digital Transformation

Incepted in 1996, Sri Devi Group (SDG) is having 25 years of experience in sales and distribution of various international brands like Adidas, Reebok, Levi’s, Lee, and Wrangler. Pandemic and lockdowns dried down the footfalls in the shopping malls and exclusive retail outlets. SDG being a pioneer wants to foray into an online business model which is a logical expansion but they are stuck with fear of unknowns that lead them into perplexing conundrums.
OurKadai team enlightened Nandakumar, Retail Director of SDG about the paradoxes around ecommerce and demystify them by breaking into easily conceivable vignettes. Prototyping, Proof of Concepts encompassing ecommerce website, back office support, inventory and Accounting were presented that showed how exactly the solution would be in play post-implementation.
We impart fishing skills to our partners than getting them a fish. That is our philosophy of partnership. In trusted partnerships, our success endures.

E-Commerce Enablement

Dollar EH, Canada

Dollar EH is a fast-growing dollar store brand in Canada, known for offering affordable everyday essentials—ranging from home goods to toys and stationery. While their physical presence was strong, they needed a digital transformation to match the modern shopper’s expectations and expand their reach across Canadian provinces.
OurKadai partnered with the Dollar EH team to craft a tailored e-commerce experience. We broke down the project into clearly defined deliverables: responsive design, intuitive navigation, Canadian tax and payment integrations, real-time inventory management, and secure checkout flow.
We believe in enabling our partners with the tools and clarity to succeed beyond launch. With a shared focus on long-term value and customer satisfaction, our collaboration delivered both reach and resilience in a competitive market.

Academic Upskilling

VET Group of Institutions, Erode

VET Institute of Arts and Science collaborated with OurKadai to uplift the programming proficiency of their second-year students through an intensive 100-hour Java workshop. The initiative focused on bridging the gap between textbook knowledge and real-world development skills.
The training emphasized hands-on learning with modules covering Java fundamentals, advanced concepts like Streams, Lambdas, JDBC, and software engineering practices such as TDD and version control. Students formed teams, built real-time mini-projects, and maintained GitHub repositories to showcase their work.
This academic-industry collaboration empowered 63 students with the confidence to build, collaborate, and contribute like real developers—helping them take the first big step from classroom learning to industry-readiness.
